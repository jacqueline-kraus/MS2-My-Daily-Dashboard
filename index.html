Frequently Asked Questions
What if I don't like what I see?

Just hit refresh and you get a new joke, movie or meal recommendation. As we unfortunately cannot change the weather, if we do not like it, this does not work for the weather report .


How can I change my location to see the weather accordingly?

Just click on "change location", type your new location and click the submit button.


Note:

If you keep the "My Daily Dashboard" page open in your browser, it will refresh every 24hours automatically.
